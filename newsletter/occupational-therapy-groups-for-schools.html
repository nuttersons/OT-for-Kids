I am writing in hopes of speaking to the appropriate individual who handles developing the needs of children with special educational needs and disabilities.

OT for Kids help improve the key skills needed to meet the high expectations for children at school. We have already helped over 300 children in our groups, across 9 schools in the U.K improve their handwriting, concentration, fine motor, movement, and organisation skills since we began 2 years ago, and want to make a difference to as many children as possible.

Our Occupational Therapists provide a school with four groups across one school day to develop the skills children need to concentrate better in class and make your teaching more effective. We improve the legibility of children's writing, making marking classwork easier. We develop the fine and gross motor skills needed to participate in physical education, and we build capacity of teaching staff to better understand and develop the needs of children with SEND.

Some of the schools we currently work with include: Trinity high school, Ringway Primary School, Heald place primary school, St George's primary school and Lyng Hall high school.
